# Hook the Horizon primary editorial systems

Source boundary: `next-generation/`
Canonical branch: `main`
Runtime status: source-controlled implementation; exact installed-artifact and production behavior remain separately verified.

| Editorial system | Template | Intended slug | Reader job |
|---|---|---|---|
| Living publication front | `theme/templates/front-page.html` | site front | Enter through changing conditions and the next field decision. |
| Start Here | `theme/templates/page-start-here.html` | `start-here` | Choose by destination, species, conditions, system, access, or readiness. |
| Field Files portal | `theme/templates/page-field-files.html` | `field-files` | Navigate conditions, species, destinations, systems, and trips by decision. |
| Resource Library | `theme/templates/page-resources.html` | `resources` | Find maps, setup cards, packing systems, briefs, and updated public-safe field records. |
| Tools studio | `theme/templates/page-tools.html` | `tools` | Read, build, verify, save, print, and choose go, adjust, delay, relocate, or stand down. |
| The Field Edit | `theme/templates/page-store.html` | `store` | Shop edited field systems by role, compatibility, burden, durability, and use. |
| Research and Standards | `theme/templates/page-research-and-standards.html` | `research-and-standards` | Inspect source time, uncertainty, access, regulation, stewardship, corrections, and disclosures. |

All templates retain an editable `post-content` surface. Query-driven portals include explicit unpublished-content states. Installed preview, field-media and map mapping, current official-source contracts, functional filters, application wiring, exact package parity, responsive accessibility, and sensitive-location leakage checks remain publication-specific verification tasks.

`World` and `Worlds` are not active architecture, template-family, migration, route, or public naming terms. Legacy metadata and option keys may remain only as deprecated internal compatibility mirrors and must not render to readers or be reused by new implementation.
