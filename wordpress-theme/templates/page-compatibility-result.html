Why this result exists

The setup card must distinguish manufacturer facts, declared inputs, deterministic rule findings, condition-sensitive interpretation, unknowns, and field-test steps. AI text is not evidence and cannot override a rating stop.

Before use

  • Confirm current regulations, weather, water, lawful access, and safety conditions.
  • Test drag, line lay, knots, guides, and terminal security under a safe low load.
