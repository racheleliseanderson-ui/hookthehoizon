Compatibility Builder / explainable field tool

Verify current regulations, water, weather, access, safety, and manufacturer limits before acting. The tool must never expose exact private locations or restricted-access notes.

1. Water and conditions

  • Broad water type and current state
  • Visibility, temperature, flow, and weather
  • Public access and safety constraints
  • Condition timestamp and freshness

2. Target and technique

  • Species or presentation goal
  • Depth and cover
  • Retrieve or drift requirements
  • Regulation-sensitive constraints

3. Gear system

  • Rod power, action, and length
  • Reel capacity and drag range
  • Main line and leader
  • Lure, hook, weight, or terminal limits

Validation state

Do not produce a recommendation when required dimensions conflict, regulation freshness is unknown, safety limits are missing, or the requested system exceeds a component rating.

Decision method

The builder evaluates the system as a chain. A recommendation must explain which component constrains the setup, which inputs are assumptions, and what must be confirmed in the field.

  1. Reject unsafe or prohibited combinations.
  2. Identify the weakest compatible component.
  3. Match presentation requirements to water and target.
  4. State uncertainty, freshness, and limitations.
  5. Return a printable setup card without protected location data.

Empty and error states

Missing data: request the absent field and explain why it affects compatibility.

Conflicting ratings: identify the conflict and block the result.

Stale regulation or conditions: mark the result unavailable until refreshed.

Privacy boundary

Public output may use broad region, water type, season, and generalized conditions. Exact coordinates, private access instructions, gate combinations, landowner details, and sensitive notes are prohibited.
