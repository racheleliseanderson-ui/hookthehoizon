Compatibility Builder / explainable field tool

Verify current regulations, water, weather, access, safety, and manufacturer limits before acting. The tool does not collect exact private locations or restricted-access notes.

[hth_system_compatibility_resilient]

How the decision works

  1. Reject invalid inputs and protected-location data.
  2. Stop on declared manufacturer-rating conflicts.
  3. Identify condition-sensitive trade-offs and missing evidence.
  4. Show the weakest supported links and confidence basis.
  5. Return a local, printable setup card with no location data.

Useful result states

Compatible: declared ratings align and no material condition is unresolved.

Test before use: the system may work, but identity, field conditions, or connection behavior needs confirmation.

Mismatch: a declared rating or capacity conflict must be corrected rather than averaged away.

Privacy boundary

Public output may use water type, season, and generalized conditions. Coordinates, private access instructions, gate combinations, landowner details, and sensitive notes are prohibited.
