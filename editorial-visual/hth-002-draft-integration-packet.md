**Fishing information is a layered decision system, not a single map pin.**

The image should show a practical pre-trip decision surface: regulation source, land-manager/access check, weather or water-condition check, safety equipment, and conservation step. The visual must communicate sequence and evidence boundaries rather than scenery, catches, trophies, or gear status.
