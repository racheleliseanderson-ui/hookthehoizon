**Standard:** Shared and Individual DNA Standard v1.0.0

## Core contract

**Question:** What does the water, field evidence, and actual use support?  
**Promise:** Help anglers read water, prepare intelligently, configure compatible gear, test claims honestly, and learn from what worked, failed, and changed.

## Required rhythm

Water or horizon condition → route and access context → field setup → action and evidence → close material detail → finding and limitation → next-water question.

## Motion grammar

Cast trajectory, current, route, contour, weather shift, field notation, and measured reveal. Motion remains restrained and directional; reduced motion preserves route, sequence, and evidence without transforms.

## Signature expressions

Water Read; Field Note; System Compatibility; What Held Up; Failure Record; Gear Verdict; Route Brief; Honey Hole Intelligence handoff.

## Evidence and privacy boundary

Clearly distinguish verified fact, direct observation, hypothesis, and judgment. Do not publish precise catch coordinates, live location, sensitive structure, or image geolocation. Product brands and models require primary identity evidence and honest use/testing records.

## Prohibited drift

Macho posturing; trophy-only culture; juvenile fishing identity; cartoon fish; bait-shop cliché; rustic-lodge stereotype; generic grip-and-grin imagery; hidden-location exploitation; affiliate-first gear hype.

## Acceptance

A page must show what the evidence supports, what it cannot support, and what the reader should do next while protecting sensitive locations. Apply the master `BRAND_DNA_ACCEPTANCE_CHECKLIST.md` to material changes.
