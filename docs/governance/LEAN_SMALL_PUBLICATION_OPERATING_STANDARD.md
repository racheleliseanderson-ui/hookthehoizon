# Hook the Horizon — Lean Small-Publication Operating Standard

**Status:** Active; supersedes conflicting process-heavy release and editorial gates  
**Effective:** 2026-07-20  
**Owner:** Rachel Anderson

## Purpose

Hook the Horizon is a small independent publication. Governance exists to prevent real harm and keep the site maintainable, not to imitate a large corporate compliance program.

## Default operating rule

For ordinary reversible work:

1. identify the page, post, theme, plugin, or application being changed;
2. use the canonical source when custom code is involved;
3. run the checks that matter to the changed behavior;
4. make the bounded change;
5. inspect the affected live route or function;
6. repair, deactivate, replace, or restore if a material regression appears;
7. leave a short practical record when it will help future diagnosis.

No staging environment, formal readiness packet, checksum dossier, per-change approval ceremony, repeated backup confirmation, exhaustive browser matrix, or separate legal review is a universal prerequisite.

## What still requires care

Pause only the affected action when there is a concrete risk involving:

- credentials, account access, private or protected personal information;
- exact sensitive fishing locations or access instructions;
- unsupported current regulation, access, closure, weather, water, operator, safety, medical, or legal claims that could materially harm someone;
- payment, contract, sponsorship, advertising, or other external commitment not already authorized;
- unclear ownership, consent, or publication rights;
- irreversible deletion or a destructive change without a practical recovery route.

A narrow concern does not stop unrelated work.

## Editorial standard

Ordinary articles do not need a claim ledger, legal memo, exhaustive source packet, or publication-readiness committee.

Use reliable sources for consequential or current claims. State uncertainty plainly. Link the responsible authority when rules, access, closures, weather, water, operator credentials, or species handling are material. Recheck volatile facts close to publication. Do not invent field experience, approvals, results, or local knowledge.

General educational fishing content can proceed with proportionate research and ordinary editorial judgment.

## Legal and policy pages

Privacy, Terms, disclosure, accessibility, corrections, and safety pages should be accurate, readable, and matched to the site's actual features. They should not contain invented corporate structures, arbitration programs, governing-law clauses, certifications, or operational promises.

Update them when the site's real data collection, commerce, forms, accounts, providers, or business practices materially change. Routine wording improvements do not require outside counsel. Obtain qualified advice only for a specific consequential legal question.

## Accessibility and QA

Accessibility is a product requirement, not an evidence ceremony.

Test the affected interaction: headings, labels, names, keyboard completion, visible focus, contrast, reflow, target size, status announcements, error recovery, and reduced-motion behavior where relevant. Device or screen-reader testing is useful when available, but its absence does not block unrelated minor copy, routing, or low-risk maintenance work.

## WordPress releases

- GitHub is authoritative for project-owned custom code.
- WordPress.com is the live runtime and primary recovery platform.
- WPVibe is the primary connected operating console; WP Umbrella is the secondary monitoring and maintenance console.
- Vendor-managed plugins may use vendor, host, or WP Umbrella maintenance paths.
- Project-owned custom code should use the canonical package or deployment mirror.
- Direct production work is acceptable when the change is bounded, reversible, and checked immediately.

Do not delay a useful release merely to create uniform tooling across every site.

## Records

Create a short issue comment, commit message, or operating note when a change is material, unusual, security-relevant, or likely to need later diagnosis. Do not generate recurring paperwork for routine work that completed normally.

## Supersession

Any older Hook the Horizon instruction that treats staging, exhaustive evidence, formal publication readiness, duplicate approvals, separate backup ceremonies, owner sign-off for each reversible step, or corporate-style legal review as a universal gate is superseded by this standard.

The controlling principle is: **do sensible work, check what changed, avoid real harm, and keep moving.**
