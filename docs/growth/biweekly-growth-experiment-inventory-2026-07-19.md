## Prioritized experiments
### HTH-GROWTH-01 — Tool-choice clarity
Hypothesis: A brief chooser that distinguishes compatibility, presentation planning, and intelligence lookup will improve qualified tool entry.
Status: Ready for source drafting; not authorized for live publication.
Measure: tool-entry selection and completion by tool.
Guardrails: no current-condition, access, legality, safety, conservation, availability, or crowd-level guarantee.

### HTH-GROWTH-02 — Safety and verification bridge
Hypothesis: A concise prompt to verify current access, conditions, rules, and safety before acting will increase responsible use without overwhelming the primary task.
Status: Ready for source mapping.
Measure: verification-link use and tool completion; higher clicks are not automatically interpreted as success.

### HTH-GROWTH-03 — Broad-region update retention
Hypothesis: A consent-based broad-region update can create repeat value without exposing precise locations or personal travel patterns.
Status: Design-only pending consent, source freshness, and delivery documentation.
Measure: aggregate opt-in and engagement by broad region only.
