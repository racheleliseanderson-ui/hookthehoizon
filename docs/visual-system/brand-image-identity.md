# Hook the Horizon — Brand Image Identity

This repository inherits the global authority at `Master-of-The-Universe-/docs/visual-operating-system/five-publication-image-and-visual-design-operating-system.md`.

Hook the Horizon is one equal publication implementation in the five-publication ecosystem.

## Governing question

**What is the water doing, what system fits it, and what should change next?**

## Editorial ownership

Water reading, field observation, fishing technique, equipment relationships, real-use evidence, and field-led decision support.

## Visual priorities

- Visible horizon and water behavior
- Field notation and condition context
- Equipment shown as a connected system
- Technique and presentation sequence
- Real-use evidence and limitations
- Restrained material and signal accents

## Character

Observant, capable, practical, field-led, precise, weathered, curious, and evidence-aware.

## Hard exclusions

Generic outdoor lifestyle, product-wall composition, unsupported trophy imagery, decorative wilderness filler, careless location exposure, and a recolored version of another publication.

## Motion language

Horizon movement, current direction, cast path, equipment sequence, condition change, and field notation. All motion must remain purposeful and reduced-motion safe.

## Production rule

No image or motion package begins before a complete visual brief and the applicable source, safety, provenance, and responsive-crop requirements are defined.
