## Typography
- **Bebas Neue:** display XL, H1, selected H2 and short feature labels. Never body, dense data, long warnings or small controls.
- **Montserrat:** body, H3–H6, supporting headings, navigation, buttons, labels, captions, tables, numeric displays, applications, commerce, email and print.
- No font binaries are committed. Use an approved provider/self-hosting license and `font-display: swap`.

## Logo
Use the approved primary, reverse, monochrome, monogram, favicon and social assets. Do not redraw, recolor arbitrarily, stretch, outline, shadow, bevel, animate, crop or disassemble. The owner-approved identity is distinct from prohibited decorative fishing clichés.

## Visual system
Pages follow immersion → orientation → evidence → explanation → decision → related exploration → visual rest. Use cinematic water/weather imagery, asymmetrical editorial composition, Pearl reading fields, Deep Navy authority fields, compact technical modules and deliberate dark/light transitions. Cards are only for genuinely parallel items.

## Imagery and diagrams
Every image has a job, provenance, rights state, focal point, crop family, alt purpose and location-sensitivity review. Avoid generic stock smiles, trophy-only/grip-and-grin defaults, impossible equipment/line paths, unsafe handling, fake live conditions and sensitive location disclosure. Diagrams label measured, estimated and inferred elements and provide text alternatives.

## Template and state coverage
The single register maps all global, WordPress, institutional, editorial, archive, application, WooCommerce, newsletter, error, print and alternate renderings. An unstyled default is a gap, not completion.
