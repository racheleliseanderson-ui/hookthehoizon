# Hook the Horizon Learning and Review Contract

Status: draft branch only. No publication or deployment is authorized.

## Required review fields

Every Hook draft must record young-voice review status, firsthand review status, biological-claim review status, regulation source and check date, source freshness, and location-sensitivity level.

A draft remains blocked while an applicable review is pending.

## Voice standard

Use a credible, knowledgeable 16-year-old voice: direct, curious, energetic, useful, and naturally funny. Explain technical terms and state what is known, sourced, observed, tested, or still uncertain.

Reject adult corporate prose, forced slang, childish phrasing, invented field experience, unsupported performance claims, and generic retail language.

## Living Fly Bench / Hatch Match

Maintain structured insect, forage, life-stage, fly-pattern, materials, substitution, observation, and confidence records. Show regional and seasonal limits. A fish response alone does not establish a species identification.

## Presentation Lab / Presentation Planner

Teach and record depth, speed, angle, line shape, current or wave action, cadence, profile, visibility, contact state, environmental condition, controlled change, result, and uncertainty.

## Rig Signal Lab / Rig Signal

Expose source specifications, ratings, paired-equipment assumptions, attachment compatibility, practical balance, presentation suitability, legal and safety limits, device-capability source, test status, failure modes, and no-purchase adaptations.

## System Compatibility

Keep these states separate: attachable, within published ratings, practically balanced, suitable for the intended presentation, safe, legal, field-tested, and plausible but unverified.

## Honey Hole Intelligence

Every water record must include broad location class, access status and authority, regulation authority, closure and hazard freshness, seasonal context, conservation sensitivity, permitted location precision, metadata-redaction rule, source confidence, and next verification date.

Do not reveal sensitive location details or identifying metadata without explicit human and conservation review.

## Education versus recommendation

Editorial education explains variables, evidence, and trade-offs. Recommendation logic uses structured verified records and must explain the result, missing data, uncertainty, and a no-purchase option.

## Visible draft note

Young brand voice and firsthand review required before publication. Field outcomes, local conditions, equipment-performance statements, and personal judgments remain provisional until reviewed.
