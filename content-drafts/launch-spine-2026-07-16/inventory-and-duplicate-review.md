## Evidence and review gates

- Separate official information, general background, contributor observation, inference, recommendation, and unknown.
- Record firsthand evidence as available, absent, or blocked; never invent a field result or personal voice claim.
- Verify current regulations, access, closures, hazards, weather/water warnings, and source review times.
- Apply location privacy to prose, images, metadata, exports, feeds, schema, analytics, and sharing.
- Product specifications cannot support a performance verdict by themselves.
- Every draft requires young-contributor voice review without forced slang or exaggerated expertise.
